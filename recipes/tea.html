Cup of Tea

Enjoying a cup of hot tea

Pictured above is a cup of tea using a regular tea bag for black tea with boiling water added. Some milk has also been added.

Ingredient

  • Water (brought to a boil using a kettle)
  • Black tea bag
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Fill kettle with water from tap
  2. Boil water in kettle
  3. Place tea bag in ceramic cup or cup of preference which can withstand the heat of the boiling water
  4. Pour boiling water into cup until it is around 3/4 full
  5. Stir the tea bag in the water and leave it to brew for a little while (a few minutes). Remove teabag (this can also be done at the very end, depending on preference)
  6. Pour milk into cup (the amount depends on preference but try not to fill to the very top as this can create an uncomfortable drinking experience)
